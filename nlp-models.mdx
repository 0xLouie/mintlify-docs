## NLP Models (DistilBERT, FinBERT, etc.)

Pulsr leverages compact, transformer-based language models like DistilBERT and FinBERT for classification, relevance filtering, and sentiment detection. These models have been fine-tuned for financial language using over 2 months of labeled Twitter financial content, including thousands of real market-moving tweets.
