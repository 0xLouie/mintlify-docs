## Quick Start Guide

1. Follow [@newsbypulsr](https://x.com/newsbypulsr) for AI-analyzed news updates
2. Visit [pulsr.news](https://pulsr.news) to explore all posts and filter by asset or impact level
3. Use the sentiment and impact scores to prioritize your trades
4. Join the Discord for feature requests and alerts
